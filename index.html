Mission

To cultivate grounded spaces—gardens, groves, and gentle rooms—where coherent attention can be practiced and carefully studied. We welcome truth-seekers and families alike, keeping sovereignty and safety at the center.

  • Warm, non-extractive culture
  • Accessible practices for all bodies & neurotypes
  • Open curiosity, transparent methods
Camp Nights

Our outdoor “camp night” sessions (inspired by Telepathy Camp’s playful spirit) bring the study outside: blankets, lanterns, shared timing, clear consent cues, and quiet instrumentation. It’s research, but it feels like belonging.

Dual Structure

Nonprofit core: scholarships, support circles, and assistive communication programs focused on families and non-speakers.

For-purpose services: trainings, retreats, and aligned offerings that sustain the mission and expand access—our alternative path to care.

Why this model?

It lets us hold the sanctuary while building a resilient ecosystem: community care at the center, sustainable services around it.

Methods

We blend coherence practices with careful measurement and plain-language reports. Sessions are gentle, time-bounded, and revisitable.

  • Practice: breath-led settling, shared timing, clear open/close cues
  • Measurement: event markers, low-light environments, minimal interference
  • Analysis: effect sizes, confidence intervals, replication goals

Context we watch (balanced & evolving): Ganzfeld meta-analyses and presentiment/PAA work; community-data projects like GCP.

Transparency

Protocols and summaries are published whenever possible. Participants can review their contributions by request.

Participate

Share a little about yourself and how you’d like to be involved—family support, training, or research studies. We’ll follow up with consent options and next steps.

We won’t share your info. You can opt out anytime.

Consent & Care

Participation is always opt-in. You can pause or withdraw, and you choose what gets recorded or kept private. Our culture is slow, kind, and clear.

  • Consent by design, every step
  • Minimal, purpose-limited data
  • Participant review on request

Accessibility

We adapt pace, format, and space—outdoor garden sessions, low-stim rooms, and flexible timing—to meet different bodies and brains with respect.

About

The Telepathy Center grew from a simple idea: when people feel safe, seen, and sovereign, connection becomes measurable and meaningful. We walk the line between the mystical and the methodical—trees above us, clean notes in hand.
